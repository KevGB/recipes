Soft Boiled Eggs

  1. Boil a pot of water with enough water to cover eggs 1 inch
  2. Add a Tablespoon of Vinegar and pinch of Salt
  3. Place eggs in boiling water
  4. Set timer for 4 minutes
  5. Remove
  6. Cool under cold water
  7. Peel

  • Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • vinegar
  • Salt

Eggs with Potato Lovin'

  1. Cut potatoes, put them in a pan, cover and steam in a little water
  2. Cut onions, garlic, peppers, carrots
  3. When potatoes are getting soft, add oil or butter
  4. Fry potatoes dry until starting to brown
  5. Add other veggies and cover
  6. Add spices
  7. Add cheese and cover
  8. In separate pan, fry eggs
  9. Serve side-by-side

  • Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Bell Peppers
  • Carrots
  • Cheese
  • Olive Oil or Butter
  • Rosemary
  • Spicy Pepper
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

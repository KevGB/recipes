Fruit Salad Recipe

Classic Fruit Salad

  1. Cut fruits
  2. Put into a bowl
  3. Pour Yogurt on top with a little Coconut Milk
  4. Pour Oats and Granola on top
  5. Crush Nuts and sprinkle on top
  6. Sprinkle with Cinnamon

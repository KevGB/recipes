Protein Smoothie Bowl

  1. Put some ingredients into Jussi's blender
  2. Press the button to make it go brrrrrrrrrzzzz
  3. Pour into a bowl
  4. Clean Jussi's blender
  5. Cut fruit, and put on top
  6. Pour Oats and Granola on top
  7. Crush Nuts and sprinkle on top
  8. Sprinkle with Cinnamon

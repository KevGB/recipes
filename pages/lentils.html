Lentil Soup

  1. Boil pot of water
  2. add Lentils
  3. Cut potatoes, put them in water
  4. Cut onions, garlic, peppers, carrots
  5. When potatoes are getting soft, add oil or butter
  6. Add other veggies and cover
  7. Add spices
  8. Drop in Eggs to Poach

  • Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Eggs
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Bell Peppers
  • Carrots
  • Olive Oil or Butter
  • Garam Masala
  • Spicy Pepper
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Daal

  1. Soak Lentils 4 hours
  2. Heat butter or oil
  3. add onions, garlic, ginger. Cook slowly 15 min until onions caramelize
  4. Stir in spices, add 100ml water
  5. Add tomatoes and spicy pepper
  6. Add Lentils and 400ml water
  7. Let it slow cook for 2 hrs
  8. Stir in Cream or Milk
  9. Serve with Rice or Naan
